Chicken Curry

Description

This is my go to recipe. You just put everything into a put and let it simmer. It's great to serve with rice and some broccoli. This can be eaten for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz uncooked boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1.5 T canola or vegetable oil
  • 2 T curry powder
  • One half chopped onion
  • 1 heaping t minced garlic
  • 14 ox can coconut milk
  • 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes, mashed or pureed
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 T sugar
  • 1/3 C unsalted peanuts

Steps

  1. Heat oil and curry powder in a large saucepan or pot for 2 minutes.
  2. Add onions and garlic and cook one minute more.
  3. Add chicken, tossing to coat with curry oil. Cook for ten minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk, tomatoes, tomato sauce, sugar and peanuts. Stir to combine.
  5. Let simmer for an hour.
