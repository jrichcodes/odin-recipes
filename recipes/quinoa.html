Quinoa Salad

Description

This is great for lunches and has lots of fiber for great poops. You can really put veggies, seeds, and cheese you want in it. I recommend goat or feta cheese to top it off.

Ingredients

  • 5 T lemon juice
  • 1 T honey
  • 1 t dijion mustard
  • 1 spoon garlic
  • 3/4 t salt
  • 1 t pepper
  • 2 T canola oil
  • 2 T olive oil
  • 1 cup of dried quinoa
  • whatever veggies you want to add

Steps

  1. In large pot , boil 1 cup quinoa + 2 cups water.
  2. Reduce to very low simmer, cover and cook for 15 mins.
  3. Remove from heat and let sit 5 mins
  4. Allow to cool
  5. Add in chopped veggies
  6. Make salad dressing
