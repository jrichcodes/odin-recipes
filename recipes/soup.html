Jambalaya Soup

Description

This is a great soup to spice things up in the kitchen. This recipe can be made with shrimp, chicken, andouille sausage -- or all three! It is easy to make and hearty.

Ingredients

  • 4 cup unsalted chicken stock
  • 1 cup yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 12 oz medium raw shrimp
  • 6 ounces smoked turkey sausage
  • 1 cup chopped raw celery
  • 1 tbsp salt-free creole seasoning
  • 10 fluid ounce diced tomatoes and green chilies
  • 1.75 cup petite diced tomatoes canned
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste

Steps

  1. In large soup pot, cook onion and celery and bell pepper.
  2. Add everythign but shrimp and simmer until heated thru.
  3. Add shrimp.
  4. Serve with rice
