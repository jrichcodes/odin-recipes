Beef Stroganoff

Description

The most amazing poor man's beef stroganoff you will ever have! (I use ground beef.) Golden ground beef smothered in an incredible sour cream mushroom gravy, this beef stroganoff recipe is an easy 30 minute recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cooked ground beef
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1/4 t pepper
  • 1 T butter
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 t garlic
  • 4 T flour
  • 14 oz can beef broth
  • 4-oz can mushrooms, stems and pieces, drained
  • 1/2 C sour cream

Steps

  1. In large soup pot, melt butter. Add onion and cook until softened, then add garlic.
  2. Stir flour into onion/garlic mixture. Add beef to pot, then add beef broth and mushrooms and bring to a low boil.
  3. Simmer until thickened to desired consistency.
  4. Add sour cream.
  5. Service with noodles.
